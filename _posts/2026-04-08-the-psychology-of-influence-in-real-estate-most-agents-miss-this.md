Most agents are saying the right things…

And still losing deals.

Because in real estate, it’s not just about what you say.

It’s about how you say it.<br>How you show up.<br>And how much influence you actually have when it matters.

## The Real Problem

Clients today are more resistant than ever.

They question everything.<br>They hesitate.<br>They delay decisions.

And most agents don’t know how to move them.

This isn’t a script problem.

It’s an influence problem.

## What You’ll Learn In This Training

In this session, Blake Sloan breaks down the psychology behind influence and what it really takes to guide clients with confidence.

You’ll learn:

* Why influence is not manipulation
* The 7/38/55 rule and how communication really works
* Why scripts still matter even though words are only 7%
* How tonality and body language build or break trust
* The law of certainty and why confidence drives decisions
* How to use tactical empathy to lower resistance
* Why authority is everything in today’s market
* How to position yourself as the expert clients trust

## Why This Matters

If you can’t influence the conversation, you can’t control the outcome.

And if you can’t control the outcome…

You can’t close consistently.

The agents winning right now aren’t just working harder.

They understand how people think.<br>How people feel.<br>And how to guide decisions.

## Final Thought

Influence is the foundation of everything in this business.

Master it…

And everything else becomes easier.
